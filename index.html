Classroom-ready tools

Robotics 101 Classroom Tools

Free, ready-to-use activities for grades 4 to 8. Most tools work directly in your browser. Pick one, click “Start,” and you are moving.

Microbit Challenge Generator

Generate quick coding challenges you can run as bell work, centers, or fast-finish tasks.

Grades: 4 to 6 Time: 10 to 20 minutes Setup: none
Start the Tool Teacher Guide For Builders

Arduino Diagnostic App

A quick self-check to help students (and teachers) see what they know before starting a unit.

Grades: 6 to 8 Time: 10 to 15 minutes Setup: browser
Start the Tool Teacher Guide For Builders

Mecanum Wheel Lessons

A step-by-step lesson sequence to get a basic mecanum vehicle moving and then iterating on control.

Grades: 6 to 8 Time: 1 to 2 class periods Setup: kit required
Open the Lesson Teacher Guide For Builders

Air Quality Monitor Project

A project scaffold for building a simple sensor-based monitor and learning how measurement can drift.

Grades: 6 to 8 Time: 2 to 4 class periods Setup: parts required
Open the Project Teacher Guide For Builders
